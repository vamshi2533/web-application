How It Works

Image Recognition

CogniScan processes uploaded brain MRI images, recognizing distinct features and patterns associated with different types of brain tumors.

Classification Output

After analyzing the uploaded image, CogniScan provides a clear output indicating the type of brain tumor detected, including glioma, meningioma, pituitary tumor, or no tumor detected.

Real-Time Results:

Users receive instant feedback as CogniScan swiftly analyzes the uploaded image, offering actionable insights that aid healthcare professionals in making timely and informed decisions regarding patient diagnosis and treatment.
